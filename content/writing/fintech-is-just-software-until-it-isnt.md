When money moves, software stops being purely digital. Every bug has a real-world cost, and every latency spike is someone waiting for their paycheck.

This post explores what changes when correctness and accountability become non-negotiable. It covers operational realities, irreversible workflows, and the need to design for failure from day one.

The payoff is a system that behaves predictably under stress and earns trust from users and partners alike.
